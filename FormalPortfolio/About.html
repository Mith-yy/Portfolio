Hey there! Mithran here

I'm Mithran, a 14-year-old amateur photography enthusiast with a passion for aviation and technology—while I'm not a professional and shoot solely on an iPhone 15, I’m driven by curiosity and a growing creative eye, with a dream to one day explore and capture the natural beauty of places like Alaska. I've always strived to picture everything around me in new angles and light to bring to the people around me a perspective they never imagined and making them see my love in everyday sights around us. I also pride myself in being an intermediate level coder, mainly specialising in Lua and recently got into front-end web development. I've helped beginners find their footing and solve their bugs in their Roblox games via Discord servers, and also contributed to a game with more than 25 million visits.

As a proud student in Victoria School, Singapore I pride myself in being academically-strong while also being able to explore new interests and skills. Outside of technology and photography, i enjoy playing various sports like floorball, football and basketball. I'm often surrounded by like-minded Victorians, ambitious and hardworking. Since young, I've always been more outgoing than the rest, willing to offer a helping hand. I hope to continue what I'm doing, network with more people and progress in not only skills but in character.
